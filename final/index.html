  • In creating the LightUp game, my focus was on crafting a captivating puzzle experience with a polished user interface. I incorporated smooth transitions and hover effects to elevate the visual appeal, aiming to immerse players in an engaging gameplay environment. However, the journey was not without its challenges. Debugging JavaScript logic and ensuring cross-device and cross-browser compatibility proved to be significant hurdles, demanding meticulous attention and problem-solving skills.
  • Throughout the development process, I gleaned valuable insights. Foremost among them was the recognition of the iterative nature of game design. Constant refinement through successive iterations allowed for the fine-tuning of gameplay mechanics and user interface elements. Additionally, I came to appreciate the pivotal role of user testing in validating design decisions and identifying areas for improvement.

By combining creative design choices with rigorous testing and iterative development, LightUp emerged as a compelling and visually captivating game, ready to engage players across various platforms.
